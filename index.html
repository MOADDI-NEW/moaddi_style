Who We Are

Our Brand (Moaddi) the well-known brand in Saudi Arabia that people trust to make this brand strong in the Middle East

Moaddi, a trusted brand in Saudi Arabia, leverages vending machines to bring quality products closer to people.
As Moaddi expands, our vending machines are key to building a strong presence across the Middle East.
Moaddi’s well-known vending solutions reinforce our brand’s reliability and trust across Saudi Arabia and beyond.

Our Services

Moaddi Marketing Line provides integrated solutions for all stakeholders in the field of vending machines.
For customers , we provide a variety of products easily available through vending machines in multiple locations, which contributes to saving their time and effort to reach their daily needs quickly and easily.
As for marketers , we provide them with an integrated platform that enables them to build fruitful relationships with companies wishing to market their products through vending machines, with continuous support to ensure the success of their partnerships.
We also provide companies with a valuable opportunity to reach new markets through a wide network of vending machines, allowing them to display their products in strategic locations and build direct relationships with customers.
Moaddi seeks to make the buying and selling process smoother and more innovative for all parties, with a focus on enhancing trust and transparency in every transaction.
